Sahih al-Bukhari - Book 1: Revelation
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Actions are judged by intentions, and every person will be rewarded according to what they intended." (Bukhari 1)

Sahih al-Bukhari - Book 2: Faith
The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Islam is built on five pillars: Testimony that there is no god but Allah and Muhammad is His Messenger, establishing prayer, paying Zakat, fasting in Ramadan, and pilgrimage to the House for those who can afford it." (Bukhari 8)

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself." (Bukhari 13)

Sahih al-Bukhari - Book 4: Ablution
The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "The key to Paradise is prayer, and the key to prayer is purification." (Bukhari 4)

Sahih al-Bukhari - Book 30: Fasting
The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "When Ramadan begins, the gates of Paradise are opened, the gates of Hell are closed, and the devils are chained." (Bukhari 1899)
